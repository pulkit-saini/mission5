Why You’re Special

Your smile heals me
Whenever the world felt heavy, especially in the middle of the night or during my darkest storms, I would find myself scrolling through our photos. Your “Pookie selfies,” where you smiled so beautifully and genuinely — those smiles were like a quiet light in the darkness. I don’t know why exactly, but seeing your happiness lifted something inside me. Your joy became my refuge, a gentle reminder that even when everything else felt chaotic, your happiness could calm the storm within me. Your smile is a healing balm to my heart, the kind of warmth that nothing else can replace.
You never gave up on me, even when I didn’t deserve it
I know I’ve made mistakes — more than I’d like to admit. I know I wasn’t always easy to be with. And yet, through every moment I stumbled, through every silence I created, through every time you must have felt angry or hurt, you didn’t walk away. You’re still here. I know you’re mad sometimes, and that’s okay — it’s human. But you didn’t give up on me. You stayed, even when it was hard. That alone speaks volumes about your strength, your love, and your heart. I see you. I know you. And that means more to me than words can say.
You’re home to my heart
When the whole world seemed to kick me down, when doors closed and I felt lost and rejected, you were the one who accepted me — fully, without conditions. You saw my flaws and my fears and still chose to hold me close. Your acceptance is the safest place I know. Even now, after everything, you still accept me — the real me — and that’s a gift I cherish deeply. In a world full of uncertainty, your love is the place where I find peace, belonging, and true home.
You inspire me to be better
You challenge me, not by pushing me, but by believing in me. Your faith in what I can become pushes me to grow, to learn, to heal. Because of you, I want to be a man worthy of your love — someone who can stand beside you with strength and tenderness both. You were there pushing me when I doubted myself, when I gave up on me, when I felt unworthy — you were always there, pushing me forward. Even now, you’re guiding me on this spiritual journey, encouraging me to rise higher than I thought possible.
You’re the calm in my chaos
When my thoughts spiral or my emotions run wild, your presence is a grounding force. Just hearing your voice, or knowing you’re there, brings a calmness that nothing else can. You are the quiet harbor I return to when the storms rage inside me.
These are just a few reasons — there are millions more reasons why you are so incredibly special to me.
